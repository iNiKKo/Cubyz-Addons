Industrial Plasteel

A extension to Materialz that adds a new metal called Industrial Plasteel

⬇ Download

Industrial Plasteel is a durable but tricky metal to work with, perfect for those who like trying new combinations for tools!

Recipes:

Sulfur, Zinc Ingot -> 2 Zinc Sulfide
4 Aluminum Ingot, 3 Coal -> 7 Aluminum Carbide
7 Aluminum Carbide, 2 Zinc Sulfide -> Unstable Compound
2 Steel Ingot, Unstable Compound -> 2 Plasteel Compound
4 Sulfur, Plasteel Compound -> Industrial Plasteel
